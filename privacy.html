Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Weather AC! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information when you use our website and services. By accessing or using our website, you consent to the data practices outlined in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please refrain from using our website.

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy applies to all users, including those who simply browse the website and those who register for advanced features.

1. Information We Collect

We collect both personal and non-personal information from users of our website. The type of information we collect depends on how you interact with our website.

  • Personal Information: When you register for an account, we may collect personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment information.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use our website, including browsing behavior, search queries, IP address, device information, and other technical data.
  • Cookies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. These technologies help us remember your preferences, improve functionality, and analyze usage patterns.
  • Third-Party Integrations: If you access third-party services through our website (such as social media logins), we may collect data from those services according to their privacy policies.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for various purposes, including:

  • To provide and maintain our services, including weather updates and personalized features.
  • To process your account registration, payments, and other transactions.
  • To improve, customize, and enhance your user experience on our website.
  • To analyze usage patterns and trends to improve the functionality and content of our services.
  • To send you important notices regarding your account, updates to our services, and other promotional communications.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of use.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as outlined below:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party companies that assist us in providing services, such as payment processors, customer support, and analytics providers. These providers are obligated to protect your data and only use it for the purposes for which it was shared.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We use a combination of encryption technologies, firewalls, and secure server environments to ensure the safety of your data.

However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You have certain rights regarding the personal information we collect, including:

  • Access: You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: If your personal information is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request corrections.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the details provided in the "Contact Us" section.
